Day 6 : Media Tags | Multi Page Website

Topics Covered:

  1. Media Tags
  2. For YouTube
  3. For audio
  4. For video
  5. Professional for audio.
  6. Professional for video
  7. Multi Page Website




  1. Media Tags

    image, audio, video, YT tags, <iframe src="link"></iframe>

  2. For YouTube

    Replace "watch?v=" with "embed/"

  3. For audio

    <audio src=" " controls ></audio>

    When "youtube.bg/", replace just with "youtube.com/embed/"

  4. For video

    <video src=" " controls></video>

    Attributes - allowfullscreen (makes vid fullscreen).

  5. Professional for audio

    <audio controls>
    <source src=".mp3" type="audio/mp3">
    <source src=".ogg" type="audio/ogg">
    Your browser doesn't support audio file
    </audio>

  6. Professional for video

    <video controls autoplay>
    <source src=".mp4" type="video/mp4">
    <source src=".mov" type="video/mov">
    </video>

    Controls: gives life to audio/video

    Autoplay: helps play them

  7. Multi Page Website

    Use:
    1. & copy to get ©
    2. & reg to get ®
    3. & trade to get ™
    4. & euro to get €

    We can do it by linking each pages in each page. Preferrably in header.
    You can navigate in different pages by anchoring hem in an unordered list.
    Footer consists of info & copyrights
    Paste all the constant elements/pages in all the pages

  8. Deploy

    Can be done on Netlify
